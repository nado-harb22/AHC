About Akkar Hunting Club

Our Mission

Founded in 2022, Akkar Hunting Club is dedicated to promoting sustainable hunting practices that respect wildlife and preserve natural habitats. We believe hunting can be a force for conservation when practiced responsibly.

Our Values

Conservation

We actively participate in wildlife conservation programs and habitat restoration projects.

Education

We provide training on ethical hunting practices, firearm safety, and wildlife management.

Community

We foster a community of hunters who share knowledge, experiences, and respect for nature.
